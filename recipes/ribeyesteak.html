Ribeye Steak

Description

The perfectly seared ribeye for any ocassion, whether it's a date night in, poker night, or a lonely night of beer, ribeye, and netflix.

Ingredients

  • one 16oz ribeye steak, about 1.5 inch thick
  • Olive oil
  • half a garlic, peeled and rinsed
  • salt
  • pepper
  • salted butter
  • thyme

Steps

  1. Step 1. Heat a 10 to 12 inch skillet in oven to 500F. Remove rib eye out of refrigerator and bring it to room teperature.
  2. Step 2. Once the oven reached 500F, remove the skillet ouf ot he oven and place it o n the stove over medium-high heat for 5 minutes. Lightly coat steak with oil and season both sides with a large pinch of kosher salt and ground black pepper.
  3. Step 3. Instantly lay the rib eye in the center of the hot skillet. Sear 30 seconds, don't move the ribeye. Turn over the steak with tongs and sear and additional 30 seconds, then place the skillet straight forward into the oven for 30 2 minutes. turn the ribeye over, cook for an addition 2 more minutes.
  4. Step 4. Place a pat of herb butter on top of ribeye, take the steak out from skillet, cover loosely with foil, rest for 2 imnutes as butter melts.
